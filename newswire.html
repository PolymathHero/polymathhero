Navigating Challenges: Sound, Sizing, and Engagement on TikTok

As we embark on our TikTok journey, we're facing decisions that directly impact the user experience. Here are some challenges we're currently addressing:

1) Sound Dilemma: Contemplating whether to add sound to our TikTok photos. The auditory element adds a new layer but requires careful consideration for an enhanced user experience.

2) Sound and Engagement: Exploring the correlation between sound and engagement. Does the inclusion of sound elevate the viewer's interaction with our content? Considering the nuances of the TikTok algorithm and its preference for audio-rich content.

3) Sizing Inconsistencies: Wrestling with the challenge of varied original garment photo sizes leading to design inconsistencies. Resizing to the TikTok-friendly 9:16 aspect ratio through tools like Canva poses a unique design challenge.

4) The Quest for Sales: Determining the number of impressions/views needed to secure our first Ko-fi sale. Understanding the TikTok landscape and its impact on our sales goals.

5) Insights into Impressions: Investigating whether TikTok provides insights into impressions or views for both videos and photo thumbnails. Unveiling the metrics that guide our content strategy.

Join us as we navigate these challenges, striving to enhance our TikTok presence and provide a seamless shopping experience. Your support fuels our journey!

Refining Our Standards: Criteria for ChicAffair™ Product Selection

As ChicAffair™ continues to shape its identity in the world of online fashion, we're excited to announce our initial criteria for selecting products—criteria that focus on elevating the customer experience and maintaining a consistent, clear aesthetic across all platforms.

Our first criterion emphasizes the importance of sizing clarity. Recognizing the frustration customers face with inadequate sizing information, we commit to only featuring women's clothes on our platform that come with a comprehensive sizing chart. This decision stems from our experience with many dropshipping websites that often neglect this essential element, causing immediate hurdles for potential buyers.

The second criterion speaks to our dedication to visual consistency. Each product we showcase will stand alone, elegantly displayed against a white background. This choice is a departure from images laid on beds or worn by mannequins or models. Our invisible human, standing upright, allows for a seamless and standardized presentation that enhances the browsing experience.

Our commitment to consistency extends to our TikTok profile, which will mirror the sleek and clean design of popular fashion brands. Every TikTok thumbnail will feature a clear image of a single garment, taking full advantage of the 9:16 aspect ratio without any artifacts or remnants. This presentation ensures that our audience experiences a visually cohesive journey through our curated collection.

Design Dilemma: We are currently deliberating on whether to add text, such as product titles and prices, to our TikTok thumbnails. The challenge lies in the manual editing process, which not only consumes time but also demands meticulous attention to detail. Hand-editing each photo raises concerns about maintaining a consistent design across our TikTok profile. Additionally, the lack of automation creates inconsistencies within individual posts, especially those featuring multiple images. We are actively exploring solutions to streamline this process while upholding our commitment to a polished and standardized presentation.

Introducing ChicAffair™: Elevating Women's Fashion on TikTok

Polymath Hero™ proudly launches its new ChicAffair™ brand, dedicated to providing clear pictures of garments on white backgrounds. Our commitment goes beyond mere curation; we leverage advanced social media research technologies, including reverse-image searches on popular AliExpress products and the discovery of dropshipping stores already selling these items.

This intelligence gathering encompasses determining hot trends and assessing the availability of high-quality photos for these garments. The acquired data will be strategically employed across our brand, enhancing the visual appeal of our curated collection.

We will utilize these higher-quality images, sourced from the internet, to showcase recently-found popular products. Each item will undergo thorough evaluation to assess its viability for listing on our TikTok profile and subsequent sale on our Ko-fi shop.

Stay tuned for updates on our TikTok journey, and follow us here.

Addendum: Our current TikTok profile username is @thepolymathhero, a decision made for another purpose less than 30 days ago. We've since reconsidered and anticipate changing our TikTok profile username to @chicaffair if available. If not, we will adjust our brand's name to align with a suitable TikTok username.
