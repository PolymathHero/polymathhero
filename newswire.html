Updates on our Public Persona Video Live Program

As we move forward with the development of our Public Persona Video Live Program, we want to share some key details about its features and initial rollout:

Web Application Launch: The program will kick off as a web application, allowing users to engage in transformative video conversations on their desktops or laptops. If successful, we will explore the implementation of a mobile app in the future. We believe that web apps provide a fundamental and flexible foundation for this innovative initiative.

Video Call Duration: Users will be allocated a minimum of 10 minutes per video call, with a maximum duration of 15 minutes. Dropping a call before the 10-minute mark will result in a one-week ban from the service. We encourage users to fully embrace the experience and engage meaningfully.

Security Measures: To ensure a safe and respectful environment, we may limit VPNs or non-controlled connections. Privacy is a priority, and we aim to strike a balance between maintaining service quality and respecting user privacy. We're actively exploring technologies that underlie VPNs and other security methods to optimize this balance.

Access to Vulnerable Populations: Unlocking access to vulnerable populations, such as users under 18, may involve additional security measures like credit card verification, phone number verification, email address verification, and record-keeping of government-issued IDs and user selfies. This is in addition to our standard recording of every video call and quality control mechanisms.

Age Limitation: In the initial stages, the service may be limited to users over the age of 18. We are committed to creating a responsible and secure platform for all participants.

Understanding HTTP Requests: As part of our ongoing efforts to enhance our website, we are delving into the fundamentals of HTTP requests. These requests play a crucial role in the functionality of our platform, and our exploration aims to optimize user experience and platform efficiency.

We appreciate your enthusiasm and support as we navigate these developments. Stay tuned for more updates!
